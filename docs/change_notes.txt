== Update: MAAWS ballistics fix + field-manual pass ==

FIXED — maaws: the unguided HEAT 665 CS and HE 448 AB left the tube at 230 / 255 m/s, but the vanilla MRAWS FCS optic reticle is calibrated to the vanilla 350 m/s muzzle velocity — so they dropped ~150 m short (a 400 m aimpoint impacted ~250 m). Both now launch at 350 m/s (initSpeed in the magazine + maxSpeed in the ammo), matching the vanilla trajectory the reticle expects; drag (airFriction 0.05) and mass are unchanged, so the ballistic natures now hit on the aimpoint. ADM 484 PROX keeps its lower velocity — it is aimed by its scripted proximity fuze, not the reticle.

NEW — map Notes briefing: added a "Vehicle & aircraft missiles" page for the missiles component (Broadsword, Wraith, Warhammer, Hydra-P, Copperhead, Sentry, Roadrunner) — the family had no diary entry.

CHANGED — map Notes briefing: added employment ("Use") lines to every page where a round needs more than point-and-shoot — MAAWS, Vorona / RPG, 40mm ISR/EW, Counter-UAS PAB, medium autocannon and tank main gun — plus a global airburst-control note in the Overview: the shared Mk364 "Cycle Airburst Range" keybind is UNBOUND by default (bind it under Options > Controls > Configure Addons > Ghosts of Battle, or use the ACE self-interaction menu; steps 0 / 100 / 150 / 200 / 250 / 300 / 400 m, 0 = off).

== Update: QAV / VVE vehicle compat addons ==

NEW — five compat addons that wire the existing FA magazines onto vehicle-pack
weapons. All are pure wiring (magazines[] += on the weapon/muzzle class, never = ,
so they never wipe another addon's mags), define no new ammo of their own, and
set skipWhenMissingDependencies = 1 so each self-skips when its target mod is absent:

- ghostfa_vve (requires vve_core) — VVE_Core vehicle weapons: 30mm RCWS (APFSDS/API/
  HEI/ABM/PROX), 35mm AHEAD, both .50 HMGs (Mk211/Mk258 belts), 105mm MGS gun
  (cannon_105mm + vve_cannon_105mm), and the Titan launcher fed the count=1 FA
  missile variants (Broadsword/Copperhead AT + Sentry/Roadrunner AA).
- ghostfa_vehicles (requires A3_Weapons_F) — shared VANILLA vehicle secondaries, so
  the belts reach every A3 vehicle using them: LMG_coax / LMG_RCWS (7.62 M80A2 +
  XM751), MMG_02_coax / _amv (.338 Mk372), HMG_127_MBT / _APC / _AFV (.50 Mk211 +
  Mk258). Covers the Marshall coax + ATGM .338 and the Rhino coax + commander HMG.
- ghostfa_qav_abramsx (requires QAV_AbramsX) — 120mm M360, 30mm M914 (HighROF muzzle)
  + the twin's own HighROF, .338 + .50 coax, .50 Kongsberg RWS, 7.62 XM134 minigun.
- ghostfa_qav_ef_abramsx (requires QAV_EF_AbramsX_Compat) — re-asserts the 30mm FA
  appends AFTER the EF compat, which overwrites (=) the M914 HighROF magazines to
  inject its EF HEAB round; load order via requiredAddons keeps both alive.
- ghostfa_qav_ripsaw (requires QAV_Ripsaw) — mk30 30mm split by muzzle (kinetic AP:
  APFSDS+API / HE: HEI+ABM+PROX), .50 M2 RWS, 7.62 coax. These are QAV-specific
  subclasses that redefine magazines[] with = , so the shared-class patches can't
  reach them — each needs its own += here.
Marshall needs no dedicated addon: its weapons are covered through ghostfa_vve
(vve_ classes) + ghostfa_vehicles (vanilla LMG_coax/RCWS).
